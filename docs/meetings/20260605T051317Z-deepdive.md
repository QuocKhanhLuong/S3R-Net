## External Signals (Researcher)

- Dual-teacher distillation is established in medical segmentation; S3R-SCSD needs confidence, agreement, and routing masks rather than naive teacher averaging.
- Boundary-specific KD and boundary/body separation already have precedent, so the defensible novelty is S3R-specific spectral-band routing and explicit state alignment.
- Frequency-domain KD supports the planned spectral boundary and state KD losses, especially mid/high-band amplitude alignment.
- Medical Mamba/SSM segmentation is crowded; claims should stay focused on spectral retention and state-space-inspired design unless true selective-scan SSMs are added.
- ACDC reporting should stay patient-level with per-class RV/MYO/LV Dice and surface metrics.
